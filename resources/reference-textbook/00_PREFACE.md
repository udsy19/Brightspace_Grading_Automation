### **2. The DRIVER Framework: Your GPS for Success**

Think of DRIVER as your GPS for navigating financial problem-solving with AI. You input where you want to go, it shows you the best route, guides you turn-by-turn, and helps you avoid common pitfalls:

- **D** - **Define & Discover:** "Where would you like to go?" - Set clear financial objectives
- **R** - **Represent:** "Here's your route overview" - Plan your analytical approach
- **I** - **Implement:** "Start driving, follow the blue line" - Build with AI as co-pilot
- **V** - **Validate:** "You're on the fastest route" - Verify your solutions work correctly
- **E** - **Evolve:** "Found a faster route?" - Optimize and improve continuously
- **R** - **Reflect:** "Save this route?" - Document and share your successes

Just as GPS transformed navigation from maps and guesswork to systematic guidance, DRIVER transforms learning from memorization to systematic creation.
